# Craft Playbook

A living, append-only log of concrete improvements to how this company writes books and blog posts, kept by the R&D department (`.claude/agents/rd.md`). Every entry here is something the Ghostwriter, Editor, and CMO are expected to actually apply going forward — this file is required reading for all three before they start their work each cycle.

Format per entry: date, what was researched, the one concrete change adopted, and why. R&D also directly edits `.claude/agents/ghostwriter.md`, `.claude/agents/editor.md`, or `.claude/agents/cmo.md` when a practice should be baked into their standing instructions rather than just logged here — this file is the audit trail of *why*, those files are the audit trail of *what changed*.
