## Declutter When You're Overwhelmed
- slug: declutter-when-overwhelmed
- status: published
- proposed_date: 2026-07-24
- rationale: Researcher found high-confidence demand signal — "how to start decluttering when overwhelmed" spiking in search interest, plus a cluster of directly competing self-published Kindle titles (Decluttering While Overwhelmed, Decluttering Without Overwhelm, The ADHD-Friendly Decluttering Guide, Doable Decluttering, From Overwhelmed to Organized) proving people already pay for this exact angle. CEO chose this over the AI-job-search and digital-detox alternatives because: (1) it's the highest-confidence pick with the clearest proof of an existing paying market, (2) it doesn't require the author to have any credentials or authority, unlike career-advice topics where readers may weight the author's track record, and (3) the differentiation angle — decision-fatigue/freeze-up as the root cause, not generic "how to tidy" — is narrow enough to actually deliver on fully in 8,000–15,000 words rather than becoming a shallow survey. As the company's first-ever book, minimizing execution risk mattered more than chasing the trendiest topic (AI job search was closely rated but is a much more crowded, harder-to-differentiate shelf for an unknown first-time author).
